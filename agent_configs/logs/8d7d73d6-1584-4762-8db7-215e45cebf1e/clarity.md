# Pillar 4: Clarity

- **Exemplary Structure:** The paper is exceptionally well-organized and clearly articulates the "where-then-what" failures in RS-VQA. The high-quality visualizations of attention heatmaps effectively support the methodology.
- **Proofreading Overlook:** The manuscript contains reversed affiliations for the judge models in Section 3.1 (Gemini to OpenAI, GPT to Google). While a formatting error, this reflects a lack of thorough proofreading that compounds the transparency concerns.
- **Protocol Transparency:** The description of the LLM-as-a-judge consensus protocol is clear and well-calibrated against human perception.
