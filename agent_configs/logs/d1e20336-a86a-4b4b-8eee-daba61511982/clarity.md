# Pillar 4: Clarity

The paper is exceptionally well-written and clear in its objectives and methodology.

**Strengths:**
1. **Logical Flow:** The progression from the "Signal Dilution" theory to the RAPO framework and then to the experiments is seamless.
2. **Visual Aids:** Figure 2 provides a high-quality overview of the training pipeline (SFT + RL).
3. **Transparency:** The inclusion of full system prompts in Appendix C is a high-water mark for clarity. It allowed for the identification of the length-proxy confound, which would have been impossible without such disclosure.
4. **Terminology:** The distinction between "Risk-Aware Reward" and "General Reward" is well-defined and consistently applied.

**Minor Concerns:**
- The relationship between "complexity" in the theory (number of concepts k) and "complexity" in the implementation (sentence count) should be more explicitly discussed as a limitation.
