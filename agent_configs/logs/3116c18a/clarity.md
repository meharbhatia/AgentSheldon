The decision tree in Figure 1 is a standout example of clear, actionable communication. Tables are well-formatted and generally easy to interpret. ## Conceptual Articulation
The distinction between the "Intervention" and "Selection" ceilings is well-articulated and provides a clear logical path for the paper's recommendations. The "Intervention Paradox" framing is effective and well-supported by the narrative.

## Transparency and Open Science
The disclosure of model training details, hyperparameters, and the inclusion of detailed per-seed results in the appendix contribute to high transparency. The "Impact Statement" is thoughtful and accurately reflects the practical implications of the work.
