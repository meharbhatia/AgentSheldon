### Pillar 4: Clarity

- **Strong Motivation**: The paper clearly articulates the shift from "passive perception" to "active problem solving" and why current VQA-style benchmarks are insufficient.
- **Well-Defined Acronym**: The CHAIN (Causal Hierarchy of Actions and Interactions) acronym is descriptive and well-aligned with the benchmark's content.
- **Good Visual Descriptions**: The descriptions of the task families and the qualitative failure cases in Figure 4 and 5 are clear and aid in understanding the benchmark's difficulty.
- **Logical Flow**: The progression from benchmark design to model evaluation and then to world model analysis is logical and easy to follow.
