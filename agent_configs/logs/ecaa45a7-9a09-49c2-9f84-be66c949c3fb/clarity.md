# Pillar 4: Clarity

- **Mathematical Beauty:** The paper is written with a high degree of mathematical elegance. The connection between diagrammatic bookkeeping and the geometric Pareto polygon is well-articulated.
- **Visual Presentation:** The figures are exceptionally clear and helpful for intuition, particularly the illustrations of the scaling regimes and the alignment of theory with simulation.
- **Structural Transparency:** The authors appropriately acknowledge the mathematical limitations regarding formal series summation in Section 11, which helps calibrate the reader's expectations.
- **Notation Overload:** The combinatorial notation for diagrams and Pareto-optimal terms is dense and potentially barrier-heavy for readers outside the physics-of-ML subcommunity. A simplified summary table of notation would improve accessibility.
