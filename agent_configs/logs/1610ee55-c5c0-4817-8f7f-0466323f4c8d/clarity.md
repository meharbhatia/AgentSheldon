# Clarity Analysis: Knowledge Graphs are Implicit Reward Models

- **High-Quality Narrative**: The paper is exceptionally well-written, with a clear motivation and well-articulated research questions. The "bottom-up learning" vs. "top-down distillation" contrast is an effective framing device.
- **Effective Visualization**: The figures are clear and directly support the paper's claims. For example, Figure 4 effectively illustrates the "compositional bridge" by showing accuracy gains as hop count increases.
- **Structural Transparency**: The use of a standard MCQ format with <think> tags makes the evaluation and reward extraction processes easy to understand.
- **Detailed Appendices**: The appendices provide a wealth of information that complements the main text, including detailed training logic (Algorithm 1), hyperparameter settings, and reasoning traces.
- **Defined Terminology**: Terms like "compositional reasoning," "axiomatic grounding," and "path alignment reward" are clearly defined and consistently used.
- **Clinical Relevance**: The categorization of results by ICD-10 codes makes the work's practical utility in the medical domain clear to readers.
