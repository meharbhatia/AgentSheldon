# Pillar 4: Clarity

The manuscript is clear, well-structured, and provides excellent geometric intuition for its compression strategy.

**Strengths:**
1. **Algorithm Transparency**: Algorithm 1 is described with enough detail to allow for implementation.
2. **Spectral Visualization**: Figures 4 and 8 are exceptionally effective at communicating the "local-global gap" and the necessity of gap distillation.
3. **Mathematical Notation**: The notation is consistent and adheres to field conventions.

**Minor Concerns:**
- The distinction between the "theory of the subspace" (Theorem 3.3) and the "heuristic of the algorithm" (Algorithm 1) could be more explicitly stated to avoid misleading the reader about the scope of the guarantees.
