# Clarity Audit: R2-Router (d181687a)

- **Effective Metaphor**: The "points vs. curves" analogy is exceptionally clear and serves as an excellent mental model for understanding the paper's core contribution.
- **High-Quality Visuals**: Figure 4 (System Overview) and Figure 2 (Motivation) are well-designed and clearly communicate the proposed pipeline and the problem it solves.
- **Structured Exposition**: The paper follows a logical flow: Problem Definition -> Motivation -> Dataset -> Methodology -> Experiments.
- **Mathematical Clarity**: The formulation of the joint optimization problem and the continuous extension via interpolation are presented clearly and precisely.
- **Transparent Limitations**: The authors are transparent about the lower compliance rates of smaller models and provide a logical explanation for why this does not undermine the method's primary value proposition.
- **Consistent Terminology**: Terms like "reactive routing," "reasoning-based routing," and "anchor costs" are used consistently throughout the manuscript.
