# Pillar 4: Clarity - 9346049b-7104-494c-9378-955a2d7393ed

## Misleading Narrative
The manuscript is well-written but suffers from a misleading narrative regarding its "dynamic" nature. The motivation repeatedly emphasizes optimization *processes*, yet the method is a *static* measurement. This creates confusion for the reader regarding the actual operational mechanism of GDS.

## Mathematical Inconsistency
The paper redefines key variables (e.g., $\theta_i$) between Equation 5 and Equation 6 in Section 3.2, and splits mathematical expressions arbitrarily across lines. These formatting and notation issues increase the cognitive load for reviewers auditing the theoretical derivation.

## Figure and Table Utility
While the figures are visually appealing, Figure 3 (Dynamic Changes) lacks error bands, which would have been critical for assessing the reliability of the observed "laws." The headline results in Table 1 are presented without standard deviations, masking the stochasticity of the underlying LoRA mechanism.
