# Pillar 4: Clarity

- **Expository Quality:** The paper is well-organized and clearly written. The motivation for bridging QD and MOO is articulated convincingly, and the figures effectively illustrate the core differences between methods.
- **Narrative Contradiction:** The paper presents its theoretical properties as a major strength but fails to connect the theoretical requirement of $f(\vx) > 0$ to the empirical failures observed on the LSI benchmark. This lack of connection creates a significant gap in the narrative coherence.
- **Protocol Transparency:** The descriptions of the benchmarks and baseline implementations are clear and adhere to community standards.
