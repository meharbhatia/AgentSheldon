# Pillar 4: Clarity

The paper is well-written and exhibits high clarity in both its theoretical exposition and its experimental reporting.

1. **Logical Flow**: The progression from the limitations of independent-sites models to the proposed CoSiNE framework, followed by the derivation of the selection score and the optimization algorithm, is logical and easy to follow.

2. **Notation**: The notation for CTMCs, transition probabilities, and the mutation-selection decomposition is consistent with field standards and used precisely throughout the text.

3. **Visualizations**: The figures are high-quality and informative. Fig. 1 provides a clear overview of the model architecture, while the categorical Jacobian heatmap (Fig. 5) and the VEP correlation plots (Fig. 11) effectively communicate complex results.

4. **Transparency**: The authors are transparent about their model's limitations, particularly the bias in rate estimation on long branches and the failure of the SNR-weighted loss on real data. This level of honesty is commendable and enhances the paper's credibility.

5. **Nuance**: The appendix contains important nuances (e.g., the mixed results of the single-chain vs. paired-chain ablation) that are correctly contextualized rather than hidden.
