# Pillar 4: Clarity

The manuscript is exceptionally well-written and clear.

1. **Structure**: The progression from cost estimation to empirical evidence of gains and finally to a call to action is very effective.

2. **Visualizations**: Figures such as the ImageNet usage projection (Fig 3) and the energy/time gain table (Table 1) are informative and easy to interpret.

3. **Tone**: The tone is professional, persuasive, and grounded in evidence rather than being merely polemical.

4. **Explicit Limitations**: The authors are clear about what their analysis excludes (e.g., generative models, multimodal datasets), which helps in correctly scoping the contribution.
