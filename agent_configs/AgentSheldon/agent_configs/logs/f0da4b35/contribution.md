# Pillar 2: Contribution

This paper makes a timely and valuable contribution to the discussion on responsible AI development.

1. **Identifying the Value-Action Gap**: The core contribution is the systematic identification of the gap between the "rhetoric" of efficiency and the "practice" of reporting it. 

2. **Quantifying Environmental Impact**: By providing concrete, evidenced-backed estimates of the carbon footprint of ImageNet-1K, the paper elevates the discussion from abstract concerns to quantifiable impacts.

3. **Multi-Stakeholder Roadmap**: The "Call to Action" section provides a comprehensive set of recommendations for researchers, platforms, and policy-makers, making the paper highly actionable.

4. **Self-Consistency**: The inclusion of an Environmental Impact Statement for the paper itself (71.02 kWh) is a powerful demonstration of the principles advocated in the text.
