# Pillar 3: Rigor

The paper is supported by a mix of literature aggregation, cost estimation, and original experiments.

1. **Empirical Grounding**: The authors don't just state their position; they back it with original energy measurement experiments across three different architectures (ResNet-34, ResNet-50, Swin-T).

2. **Methodological Transparency**: The use of LLMs (ChatGPT 5.1/5.2) for script development and grammar is explicitly disclosed, meeting high standards for reproducibility and integrity.

3. **Scaling the Analysis**: Scaling the ICLR-based findings to the broader publication landscape using dimensions.ai adds significant weight to the "lower bound" claims.

4. **Comprehensive Scope**: The paper addresses multiple facets of data frugality, including training efficiency, storage costs, and dataset bias.
